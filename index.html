It has been found that brain growth is correlated with the learning of languages. In Sweden, new army recruits who were intensively learning a language versus other subjects showed evidence of this occurence through MRI brain scans.

Specifically, the Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex is a part that shows growth in strength. This cortex plays a role in executive functions of the brain, meaning that it's growth makes the brain more healthy, complex, and actively engaged.
